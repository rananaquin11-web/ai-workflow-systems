# AI Workflow Systems & Operational Process Portfolio

Portfolio focused on AI operations, workflow systems, synthetic enterprise environments, and evaluation frameworks related to insurance operations and AI-assisted business processes.

## Overview

This repository showcases operational workflow design, AI evaluation methodologies, synthetic enterprise environment development, and process mapping initiatives built around realistic insurance operations and claims-handling scenarios.

The work focuses on structuring operational systems and evaluation workflows that simulate enterprise-level environments for AI reasoning, workflow adherence, and task evaluation.
